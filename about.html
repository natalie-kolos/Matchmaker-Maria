A Little More About Me

My life's mission represents the culmination of a matchmaking tradition passed down through four generations of my family. This heritage has provided me with invaluable insights into the intricacies of love and relationships. I've seamlessly blended this time-honored wisdom with modern relationship psychology and innovative search techniques, creating a unique approach to matchmaking that's tailored to the demands of today's world.

My work has been recognized by esteemed media outlets such as The New York Times, Good Morning America, CBS Mornings, The Financial Times, The Today Show, Washington Post, CNN, Bustle, and Vice. These features highlight my unwavering commitment to excellence in matchmaking and my ability to stay at the forefront of the ever-evolving world of dating trends.

I'm also the co-Founder of the Matchmakers Alliance, a pioneering trade association dedicated to promoting collaboration and knowledge sharing among 450 professional matchmakers and dating coaches. Together, we're shaping the future of matchmaking, ensuring that you have access to the best expertise and resources in the industry..

Beyond my role as a matchmaker, I'm also a proud wife and mother. My own journey in love and family life has deepened my understanding of the importance of finding the right partner to share life's joys and challenges. It's a role that has not only enriched my life but also fuels my passion for helping others find the same happiness and fulfillment.
