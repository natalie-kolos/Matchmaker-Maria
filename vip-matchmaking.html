Working with Matchmaker Maria

Matchmaker Maria is your trusted partner in the world of modern dating, dedicated to helping busy professionals like you discover the love you deserve.

After you submit your profile, Maria and her experienced team of matchmakers take a close look at your confidential information and schedule a consultation with you. During this conversation, we'll dive deep into your dating history and aspirations, tailoring our services to your unique needs.

Once you become a valued client, Matchmaker Maria goes to work! She, along with her matchmaking team, scout and vet potential matches and coordinate your dates with care. After each date, she’ll collect your feedback, helping her refine the search and ensure each match aligns with your personality, lifestyle and long-term goals.
